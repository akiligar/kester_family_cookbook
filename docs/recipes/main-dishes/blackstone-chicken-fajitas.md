### Serving Options

## Planned Transformation

This dinner is designed to become Fajita Taco Salad for a mixed warm-and-cold work-box meal.

### Additional Ingredients

- Romaine
- Salsa
- Greek yogurt
- Optional shredded cheese
- Optional crushed tortilla chips

### Before Leaving for Work

1. Pack lettuce separately.
2. Pack fajita chicken and vegetables in a microwave-safe container.
3. Pack salsa, yogurt, and tortilla chips separately.

### At Work / At 2:00 AM

1. Warm the chicken and vegetables.
2. Build the salad over the romaine.
3. Add salsa.
4. Add Greek yogurt.
5. Finish with tortilla chips just before eating.

## Storage and Leftovers

- Refrigerate fajita chicken and vegetables together.
- Keep lettuce and tortilla chips separate until serving.
- Avocado is best added fresh, not packed ahead.
