## Servings and Time

-   Servings: [4 plus leftovers]{itemprop="recipeYield"}
-   Prep time: 15 minutes
-   Cook time: 10--12 minutes
-   Total time: About 40 minutes (plus charcoal lighting)

## Ingredients

### Steak

-   [1½--2 lb flank steak]{itemprop="recipeIngredient"}
-   [1 tbsp olive oil]{itemprop="recipeIngredient"}
-   [1 tsp kosher salt]{itemprop="recipeIngredient"}
-   [1 tsp black pepper]{itemprop="recipeIngredient"}
-   [1 tsp garlic powder]{itemprop="recipeIngredient"}

### Serve With

-   Chimichurri
-   Grilled asparagus
-   Corn on the cob

## Planned Transformation

### Transformation Meal

Chimichurri Steak Salad. Reserve sliced steak and extra Chimichurri for
an intentionally planned cold salad.

### Additional Ingredients

-   Romaine
-   Cucumber
-   Thinly sliced red onion
-   Leftover grilled asparagus
-   Leftover corn kernels
-   Crumbled feta (optional)

### Before Leaving for Work

1.  Slice the steak thinly.
2.  Store steak and Chimichurri separately.
3.  Pack vegetables separately.

### At Work / At 2:00 AM

1.  Toss vegetables together.
2.  Top with steak.
3.  Dress with Chimichurri and add feta if desired.

## Storage and Leftovers

-   Refrigerate steak and Chimichurri separately for up to 4 days.
-   Slice only after the steak has rested.
-   Reserve leftover grilled vegetables for the planned transformation.

## Why This Recipe Belongs

A dependable charcoal-grilled dinner that intentionally creates a fresh
second meal instead of ordinary leftovers.

## Cooking Feedback

-   2026-07-06: Direct charcoal grilling produced excellent flavor and
    texture. Cooking the asparagus during the steak's resting time
    simplified the workflow.

## Transformation Feedback

-   2026-07-06: Plan updated to include leftover asparagus and corn in
    the salad.

## What Matters

-   Slice against the grain.
-   Cook by internal temperature rather than time alone.
-   Serve Chimichurri on the side instead of over the entire steak.
