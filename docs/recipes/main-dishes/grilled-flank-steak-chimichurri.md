# Grilled Flank Steak with Chimichurri

> A simple summer grilling recipe featuring tender flank steak topped with fresh chimichurri. Designed to produce leftovers for Chimichurri Steak Salad.

## Recipe Information

| | |
|---|---|
| Yield | 4 servings + leftovers for 2–4 lunches |
| Prep Time | 20 minutes |
| Cook Time | 12 minutes |
| Total Time | About 45 minutes |
| Difficulty | Easy |
| Equipment | Grill, tongs, instant-read thermometer |

## Why We Like This Recipe

This is one of our favorite summer grilling meals. The chimichurri adds bright, fresh flavor, and the leftovers become an excellent cold steak salad for work lunches.

## Ingredients

### Steak
- 1½–2 lb flank steak
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder

### Chimichurri
- 1 cup finely chopped parsley
- ¼ cup finely chopped cilantro (optional)
- 3 tbsp red wine vinegar
- ½ cup olive oil
- 2 tbsp jarred minced garlic
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- ½ tsp crushed red pepper flakes
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Suggested Sides
- Grilled asparagus
- Corn on the cob

## Instructions

### Prepare the Chimichurri
1. Combine all chimichurri ingredients.
2. Stir well and let rest at least 20 minutes.

### Prepare the Steak
1. Remove steak from the refrigerator 20–30 minutes before grilling.
2. Pat dry, rub with olive oil, and season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.

### Grill
1. Preheat grill to medium-high.
2. Grill 4–6 minutes per side.
3. Cook to 130–135°F for medium-rare or 135–145°F for medium.
4. Rest 10 minutes.
5. Slice thinly against the grain.

Serve with grilled asparagus, corn, and generous spoonfuls of chimichurri.

## Leftovers

Reserve enough steak and chimichurri to prepare **Chimichurri Steak Salad** the following day.

## Storage

- Refrigerate steak and chimichurri separately for up to 4 days.
- Chimichurri develops even more flavor overnight.

## Recipe Notes

- Always slice flank steak against the grain.
- Chimichurri can be made several hours ahead.
- Excellent with grilled vegetables.
