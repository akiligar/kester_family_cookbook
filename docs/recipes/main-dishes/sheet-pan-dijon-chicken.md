# <span itemprop="name">Sheet Pan Dijon Chicken</span>

<span itemprop="description">A low-effort sheet pan dinner with Dijon chicken, roasted baby potatoes, and broccoli. This dinner is designed to become a Dijon Chicken Wrap for a cold handheld work-box meal. Version: 0.3.0.</span>

<meta itemprop="recipeCategory" content="Main dish">
<meta itemprop="recipeCuisine" content="American">
<meta itemprop="prepTime" content="PT15M">
<meta itemprop="cookTime" content="PT45M">
<meta itemprop="totalTime" content="PT1H">
<meta itemprop="recipeYield" content="4 servings, plus leftovers for 2 to 4 lunches">
<meta itemprop="keywords" content="chicken, sheet pan, dijon, planned transformation, work-box friendly">

## Servings and Time

- Servings: <span itemprop="recipeYield">4, plus leftovers for 2 to 4 lunches</span>
- Prep time: 15 minutes
- Cook time: 45 minutes
- Total time: about 1 hour

## Ingredients

- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">2 lb chicken breasts</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">2 tbsp Dijon mustard</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">1 tbsp olive oil</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">2 tbsp jarred minced garlic</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Juice of 1 lemon</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">1 tsp Italian seasoning</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Salt and pepper, to taste</span>

### Sides

- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Roasted baby potatoes</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Roasted broccoli</span>

## Instructions

### Prepare

<ol>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Heat the oven to 425°F.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Mix the Dijon mustard, olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Coat the chicken with the Dijon mixture.</span></li>
</ol>

### Roast

<ol>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Roast the baby potatoes for 20 minutes.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Add the chicken and broccoli to the sheet pan.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Roast another 20 to 25 minutes, until the chicken reaches 165°F.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Rest the chicken before slicing.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Reserve sliced chicken for Dijon Chicken Wraps.</span></li>
</ol>

## Planned Transformation

This dinner is designed to become a Dijon Chicken Wrap for a cold handheld work-box meal.

### Additional Ingredients

- Flour tortilla
- Romaine
- Cucumber
- Greek yogurt
- Dijon
- Lemon juice

### Dijon Yogurt Sauce

- Plain Greek yogurt
- Dijon mustard
- Lemon juice
- Black pepper

### Before Leaving for Work

1. Mix the sauce.
2. Slice the leftover chicken.
3. Assemble the wrap or pack the ingredients separately.

### At Work / At 2:00 AM

Enjoy cold.

## Storage and Leftovers

- Refrigerate chicken separately from wrap vegetables.
- Pack Dijon yogurt sauce separately if assembling later.
