# <span itemprop="name">BBQ Chicken Stuffed Sweet Potatoes</span>

<span itemprop="description">Sweet potatoes stuffed with seasoned BBQ chicken and served with buttered corn. This dinner is designed to become a BBQ Chicken Sweet Potato Bowl for a reheated work-box meal. Version: 0.3.0.</span>

<meta itemprop="cookTime" content="PT1H">
<meta itemprop="totalTime" content="PT1H15M">

<meta itemprop="keywords" content="chicken, bbq, sweet potato, planned transformation, work-box friendly">

## Servings and Time

- Servings: <span itemprop="recipeYield">4, plus leftovers for 2 to 4 lunches</span>
- Prep time: 15 minutes
- Cook time: 1 hour
- Total time: about 1 hour 15 minutes

## Ingredients

- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">2 lb chicken breasts or thighs</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">1 tbsp olive oil</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">1 tsp kosher salt</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">1 tsp black pepper</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">1 tsp garlic powder</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">1 tsp onion powder</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">1 tsp smoked paprika</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">BBQ sauce, to taste</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">4 medium sweet potatoes</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Frozen corn</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Butter, salt, and pepper for corn</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Shredded cheddar, optional</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Green onions, optional</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Greek yogurt, optional</span>

## Instructions

### Bake the Sweet Potatoes

<ol>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Heat the oven to 425°F.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Bake the sweet potatoes for 50 to 60 minutes, until tender.</span></li>
</ol>

### Cook the Chicken

<ol>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Season the chicken with olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and smoked paprika.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Grill the chicken until it reaches 160 to 165°F.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Rest the chicken, then dice or shred it.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Toss the chicken with warmed BBQ sauce.</span></li>
</ol>

### Serve

<ol>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Split the sweet potatoes and fill with BBQ chicken.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Add cheddar, green onions, or Greek yogurt if desired.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Heat frozen corn with butter, salt, and pepper.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Reserve chicken, sweet potato, and corn for BBQ Chicken Sweet Potato Bowls.</span></li>
</ol>

## Planned Transformation

This dinner is designed to become a BBQ Chicken Sweet Potato Bowl for a reheated work-box meal.

### Additional Ingredients

- Optional shredded cheddar
- Green onions
- Greek yogurt

### Before Leaving for Work

1. Dice the sweet potato.
2. Combine with BBQ chicken and corn.
3. Pack toppings separately.

### At Work / At 2:00 AM

1. Microwave until hot.
2. Add cheddar if using.
3. Top with green onions.
4. Finish with a dollop of Greek yogurt.

## Storage and Leftovers

- Refrigerate chicken, sweet potatoes, and corn together or separately.
- Pack fresh toppings separately.
