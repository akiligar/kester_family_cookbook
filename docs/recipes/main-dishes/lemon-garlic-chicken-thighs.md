# <span itemprop="name">Lemon Garlic Chicken Thighs</span>

<span itemprop="description">Lemon garlic chicken thighs served with roasted baby potatoes and broccoli. This dinner is designed to become a Mediterranean Chicken Wrap for a work-box meal. Version: 0.3.0.</span>

<meta itemprop="prepTime" content="PT15M">
<meta itemprop="cookTime" content="PT30M">
<meta itemprop="totalTime" content="PT45M">
<meta itemprop="recipeYield" content="4 servings, plus leftovers for 2 to 4 lunches">
<meta itemprop="keywords" content="chicken, lemon, weeknight, planned transformation, work-box friendly">

## Servings and Time

- Servings: <span itemprop="recipeYield">4, plus leftovers for 2 to 4 lunches</span>
- Prep time: 15 minutes
- Cook time: 30 minutes
- Total time: about 45 minutes

## Ingredients

### Chicken

- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">2 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">2 tbsp olive oil</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">2 tbsp jarred minced garlic</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Zest of 1 lemon</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Juice of 1 lemon</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">1 tsp Italian seasoning</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">1 tsp kosher salt</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">1/2 tsp black pepper</span>

### Sides

- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Roasted baby potatoes</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Roasted broccoli</span>

## Instructions

### Marinate the Chicken

<ol>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Combine the chicken thighs, olive oil, minced garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, Italian seasoning, kosher salt, and black pepper.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Marinate for 30 minutes if time allows.</span></li>
</ol>

### Roast the Sides

<ol>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Heat the oven to 425°F.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Roast the baby potatoes for 20 minutes.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Add the broccoli and continue roasting until tender.</span></li>
</ol>

### Cook the Chicken

<ol>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Grill or pan-sear the chicken thighs until they reach 175°F.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Rest briefly before slicing.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Serve with roasted potatoes and broccoli.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Reserve sliced chicken for Mediterranean Chicken Wraps.</span></li>
</ol>

## Planned Transformation

This dinner is designed to become a Mediterranean Chicken Wrap for a cold handheld work-box meal.

### Additional Ingredients

- Flour tortilla
- Romaine
- Cucumber
- Thinly sliced red onion
- Feta

### Lemon Yogurt Sauce

- Plain Greek yogurt
- Lemon juice
- Jarred minced garlic
- Salt
- Pepper

### Before Leaving for Work

1. Mix the yogurt sauce.
2. Slice the leftover chicken.
3. Assemble the wrap or keep the ingredients separate.

### At Work / At 2:00 AM

Enjoy cold.

## Storage and Leftovers

- Refrigerate chicken separately from wrap vegetables.
- Keep sauce separate until assembling if possible.
