# <span itemprop="name">Blackstone Zucchini</span>

<span itemprop="description">A simple summer zucchini side cooked on the Blackstone with olive oil and seasonings. Version: 0.3.0.</span>

<meta itemprop="recipeCategory" content="Side dish">
<meta itemprop="recipeCuisine" content="American">
<meta itemprop="prepTime" content="PT10M">
<meta itemprop="cookTime" content="PT8M">
<meta itemprop="totalTime" content="PT18M">
<meta itemprop="recipeYield" content="4 servings">
<meta itemprop="keywords" content="blackstone, zucchini, summer, side dish">

## Servings and Time

- Servings: <span itemprop="recipeYield">4</span>
- Prep time: 10 minutes
- Cook time: 8 minutes
- Total time: about 18 minutes

## Ingredients

- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">2 to 3 zucchini</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Olive oil</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Salt</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Black pepper</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Garlic powder</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Italian seasoning</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Parmesan, optional</span>
- <span itemprop="recipeIngredient">Lemon, optional</span>

## Instructions

<ol>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Slice the zucchini into planks.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Toss with olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and Italian seasoning.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Cook on the Blackstone for 3 to 4 minutes per side.</span></li>
<li itemprop="recipeInstructions" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/HowToStep"><span itemprop="text">Finish with Parmesan and lemon if desired.</span></li>
</ol>

## Storage and Leftovers

- Refrigerate leftovers for up to 3 days.
- Best reheated quickly or chopped into bowls.
